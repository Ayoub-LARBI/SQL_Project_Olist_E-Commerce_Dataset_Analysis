# Customer Reviews & Satisfaction Analysis

## Overview

This analysis evaluates customer satisfaction using review scores, written feedback, delivery performance, and category-level ratings.

---

## Overall Customer Satisfaction

| Metric                |    Value |
| --------------------- | -------: |
| Average Review Score  | 4.09 / 5 |
| Total Reviews         |   99,224 |
| Reviews with Comments |   40,977 |
| Comment Rate          |   41.30% |

### Insight

Customer satisfaction is generally strong, with an average review score exceeding 4 stars.

More than 40% of customers provided written feedback, creating opportunities for future text analysis and sentiment analysis.

---

## Review Score Distribution

| Score   | Reviews | Percentage |
| ------- | ------: | ---------: |
| 1 Star  |  11,424 |     11.51% |
| 2 Stars |   3,151 |      3.18% |
| 3 Stars |   8,179 |      8.24% |
| 4 Stars |  19,142 |     19.29% |
| 5 Stars |  57,328 |     57.78% |

### Insight

Customer satisfaction is highly positive:

* Nearly 58% of all reviews are 5-star ratings.
* Over 77% of reviews are either 4 or 5 stars.
* Only 14.7% of reviews are 1 or 2 stars.

This suggests a generally positive shopping experience across the marketplace.

| Construction Tools Tools |   4.44 |

### Insight

Book-related categories consistently achieve the highest satisfaction scores.

| Category                | Rating |
| ----------------------- | -----: |

### Insight

Large and complex products often face greater challenges related to shipping, assembly, product expectations, and delivery damage.

---

## Delivery Performance Impact

| Delivery Status | Average Rating |
| --------------- | -------------: |
| On Time         |           4.29 |
| Late            |           2.57 |

### Insight

Delivery performance is the strongest driver of customer satisfaction identified in this analysis.

Orders delivered on time receive ratings that are approximately 67% higher than late deliveries.

The difference of 1.72 review points demonstrates the direct relationship between logistics performance and customer experience.

---

## Monthly Satisfaction Trends

Review scores remained relatively stable throughout the dataset period.

Key observations:

* Most months maintained ratings above 4.0.
* Temporary declines occurred during periods of increased order volume.
* Satisfaction recovered quickly after these declines.

### Insight

Despite rapid business growth, customer satisfaction remained consistently high, indicating that operational performance scaled effectively with demand.

---

## Rolling Customer Satisfaction Trend

The 3-month rolling average remained close to 4.0 throughout the analysis period.

### Insight

The marketplace maintained stable customer satisfaction over time without evidence of long-term deterioration in customer experience.

---

## Key Findings

1. Overall customer satisfaction is strong, with an average rating of 4.09/5.
2. Nearly 58% of all reviews are 5-star ratings.
3. Successfully delivered orders receive substantially better ratings than orders with fulfillment issues.
4. Delivery speed and reliability have a major impact on customer satisfaction.
5. Book categories achieve the highest customer ratings.
6. Office Furniture and certain fashion categories experience lower customer satisfaction.
7. Customer satisfaction remained stable despite significant marketplace growth.

---

## Recommendations

### 1. Prioritize Delivery Performance

Improving delivery reliability is likely the most effective way to increase customer satisfaction.

Focus on:

* Reducing late deliveries
* Improving logistics tracking
* Enhancing fulfillment efficiency

### 2. Investigate Low-Rated Categories

Conduct deeper analysis of:

* Office Furniture
* Fashion Categories
* Fixed Telephony

to identify common customer complaints and operational challenges.

### 3. Leverage High-Performing Categories

Study highly rated categories such as Books and Luggage Accessories to identify best practices that can be applied elsewhere.

### 4. Analyze Written Reviews

Over 40,000 written reviews are available for further sentiment analysis and customer feedback mining.

### 5. Monitor Satisfaction During Peak Seasons

Periods of rapid growth and high order volume should receive additional operational attention to ensure customer experience remains consistent.
