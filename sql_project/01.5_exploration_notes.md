# Dataset Exploration Notes

## Dataset Overview

The Olist e-commerce dataset contains information about customers, orders, products, sellers, payments, reviews, and geolocation data from a Brazilian online marketplace.

### Table Sizes

| Table          |      Rows |
| -------------- | --------: |
| Customers      |    99,441 |
| Orders         |    99,441 |
| Order Items    |   112,650 |
| Order Payments |   103,886 |
| Order Reviews  |    99,224 |
| Products       |    32,951 |
| Sellers        |     3,095 |
| Geolocation    | 1,000,163 |

---

## Time Period

The dataset covers transactions from:

* First Order: September 4, 2016
* Last Order: October 17, 2018

This provides approximately two years of e-commerce activity for analysis.

---

## Order Status Analysis

Most orders were successfully delivered.

| Status      | Orders |
| ----------- | -----: |
| Delivered   | 96,478 |
| Shipped     |  1,107 |
| Canceled    |    625 |
| Unavailable |    609 |
| Invoiced    |    314 |
| Processing  |    301 |

### Observation

More than 97% of orders reached the customer successfully, indicating a generally reliable fulfillment process.

---

## Customer and Seller Distribution

### Customers

The dataset contains:

* 99,441 customers

Top customer states:

1. SP (São Paulo) – 41,746 customers
2. RJ (Rio de Janeiro) – 12,852 customers
3. MG (Minas Gerais) – 11,635 customers

### Sellers

The dataset contains:

* 3,095 sellers

Top seller states:

1. SP (São Paulo) – 1,849 sellers
2. PR (Paraná) – 349 sellers
3. MG (Minas Gerais) – 244 sellers

### Observation

São Paulo dominates both the customer base and seller network, making it the most important region in the marketplace.

---

## Product Analysis

* Total products: 32,951
* Product categories: 73

Missing category information:

* Products with category: 32,341
* Products without category: 610

### Observation

Only a small percentage of products are missing category information, so category-based analysis should remain reliable.

---

## Customer Reviews

* Total reviews: 99,224
* Reviews containing comments: 40,977
* Average review score: 4.09 / 5

### Observation

Customers generally report positive experiences. However, less than half of reviews contain written feedback, which may limit qualitative analysis.

---

## Initial Business Insights

1. São Paulo is the largest market for both customers and sellers.
2. The marketplace has a very high order completion rate.
3. Credit cards are the preferred payment method.
4. Customer satisfaction is generally strong, with an average review score above 4 stars.
5. The dataset contains sufficient product, customer, and transaction data to perform revenue, customer, product, seller, and review analysis.
