# Revenue Analysis Insights

## Overview

This analysis examines the revenue performance of the Olist e-commerce marketplace between September 2016 and October 2018.

---

## Key Metrics

| Metric              |        Value |
| ------------------- | -----------: |
| Total Revenue       | R$16,008,872 |
| Total Orders        |       99,440 |
| Average Order Value |        R$161 |

### Insight

The marketplace generated over R$16 million in revenue from nearly 100,000 orders, with an average order value of R$161 per order.

---

## Revenue Growth Over Time

| Year |  Revenue |
| ---- | -------: |
| 2016 | R$59,362 |
| 2017 |  R$7.25M |
| 2018 |  R$8.70M |

### Insight

Revenue increased significantly between 2017 and 2018. Despite the dataset only covering part of 2018, revenue had already exceeded the total revenue generated in 2017.

This indicates strong business growth and increasing marketplace adoption.

---

## Monthly Revenue Trends

Revenue generally increased throughout 2017 and remained consistently strong during 2018.

Notable revenue peaks include:

| Month    | Revenue |
| -------- | ------: |
| Nov 2017 | R$1.19M |
| Apr 2018 | R$1.16M |
| Mar 2018 | R$1.16M |
| May 2018 | R$1.15M |
| Jan 2018 | R$1.12M |

### Insight

The highest revenue month was November 2017, likely influenced by major shopping events and holiday promotions.

Revenue remained above R$1 million for multiple months during 2018, demonstrating sustained marketplace growth rather than a one-time spike.

---

## Order Volume Trends

Order volume closely followed revenue growth.

Key observations:

* Order volume increased steadily throughout 2017.
* November 2017 recorded the highest monthly order volume with 7,544 orders.
* Order volume remained consistently high throughout 2018.

### Insight

Revenue growth appears to be driven primarily by an increase in the number of orders rather than significant increases in average order value.

---

## Revenue by Order Status

| Status      |  Revenue |
| ----------- | -------: |
| Delivered   | R$15.42M |
| Shipped     |   R$177K |
| Canceled    |   R$143K |
| Unavailable |   R$126K |

### Insight

More than 96% of total revenue came from successfully delivered orders.

A small amount of revenue is associated with canceled, unavailable, and in-progress orders, which may represent payments recorded before final order completion.

---

## Cumulative Revenue Analysis

Cumulative revenue grew steadily throughout the dataset period, reaching approximately R$16 million by the end of the observation window.

### Insight

The cumulative revenue trend shows no prolonged periods of decline, suggesting healthy marketplace expansion throughout the analyzed period.

---

## Business Conclusions

1. The marketplace experienced strong year-over-year growth between 2017 and 2018.
2. Revenue surpassed R$16 million during the analyzed period.
3. November 2017 was the strongest revenue month, indicating the importance of seasonal promotions.
4. Revenue growth was supported by increasing order volume and customer adoption.
5. Delivered orders accounted for the vast majority of marketplace revenue, indicating an effective fulfillment process.

---

## Recommendations

* Increase marketing investment during high-performing seasonal periods, particularly Q4.
* Investigate the factors behind the strong performance observed in November 2017 and replicate successful campaigns.
* Continue expanding customer acquisition efforts to sustain order volume growth.
* Monitor canceled and unavailable orders to minimize revenue leakage and improve operational efficiency.
