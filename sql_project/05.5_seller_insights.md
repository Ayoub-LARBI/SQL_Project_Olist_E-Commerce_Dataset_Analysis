## Key Findings

1. São Paulo dominates the seller ecosystem, generating over 64% of marketplace revenue.
2. Revenue is concentrated among a relatively small group of high-performing sellers.
3. Most sellers are small businesses with modest sales volumes.
4. Seller productivity varies significantly across regions.
5. The marketplace depends heavily on the seller network in southeastern Brazil.

---

## Recommendations

### 1. Reduce Geographic Concentration Risk

Expand seller acquisition efforts in regions outside São Paulo to reduce dependence on a single market.

### 2. Support Small Sellers

Provide training, promotional opportunities, and marketplace tools to help smaller sellers increase sales performance.

### 3. Identify Best Practices from Top Sellers

Analyze top-performing sellers to understand successful pricing, inventory, and marketing strategies that can be shared with other sellers.

### 4. Increase Seller Retention

Since a small group of sellers contributes a large portion of revenue, maintaining strong relationships with top sellers should be a strategic priority.

### 5. Expand High-Potential Regions

States such as Paraná, Minas Gerais, and Santa Catarina demonstrate strong seller performance and may represent attractive targets for marketplace expansion.
