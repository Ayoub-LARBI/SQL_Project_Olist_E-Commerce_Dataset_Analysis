# Product Analysis Insights

## Overview

This analysis examines product performance across categories to identify the marketplace's strongest revenue drivers, highest-selling categories, and pricing patterns.

---

## Top Revenue-Generating Categories

| Category                | Revenue |
| ----------------------- | ------: |
| Health & Beauty         | R$1.26M |
| Watches & Gifts         | R$1.21M |
| Bed, Bath & Table       | R$1.04M |
| Sports & Leisure        |  R$988K |
| Computers & Accessories |  R$912K |

### Insight

Health & Beauty generated the highest revenue, contributing over R$1.25 million. Watches & Gifts closely followed despite selling significantly fewer units than some other categories.

This suggests that certain categories achieve high revenue through higher-priced products rather than sales volume alone.

---

## Best-Selling Categories by Units Sold

| Category                | Units Sold |
| ----------------------- | ---------: |
| Bed, Bath & Table       |     11,115 |
| Health & Beauty         |      9,670 |
| Sports & Leisure        |      8,641 |
| Furniture & Decor       |      8,334 |
| Computers & Accessories |      7,827 |

### Insight

Bed, Bath & Table was the most purchased category, accounting for more than 11,000 units sold.

While it leads in volume, it ranks behind Health & Beauty and Watches & Gifts in revenue generation, indicating lower average selling prices.

| Category                            | Average Price |

### Insight

Computer-related products command the highest average prices by a significant margin.

These categories represent premium product segments where even a small increase in sales volume can have a meaningful impact on revenue.

## Revenue Contribution Analysis

Top category contributions:

| Category                | Revenue Share |
| ----------------------- | ------------: |
| Health & Beauty         |         9.26% |
| Watches & Gifts         |         8.87% |
| Bed, Bath & Table       |         7.63% |

### Insight

No single category dominates the marketplace.

The top five categories account for approximately 40% of total product revenue, indicating a diversified product portfolio and reduced dependence on any single category.

---

## Revenue vs Sales Volume

A comparison of revenue and units sold reveals different category behaviors:

### High Volume Categories

* Bed, Bath & Table
* Health & Beauty
* Sports & Leisure

These categories drive growth through frequent purchases.

### High Revenue Categories

* Health & Beauty
* Watches & Gifts
* Computers & Accessories

These categories benefit from higher-priced products and stronger margins.

### Insight

The marketplace benefits from a combination of high-volume categories and premium categories, creating a balanced revenue structure.

---

## Product-Level Analysis

The highest-performing individual product generated approximately:

* R$63,885 in revenue

The top 20 products contribute a significant amount of revenue but do not dominate the marketplace, indicating that revenue is distributed across many products rather than concentrated in a few best sellers.

### Insight

The marketplace appears to have a healthy product mix without excessive dependence on a small number of products.

---

## Key Findings

1. Health & Beauty is the highest revenue-generating category.
2. Bed, Bath & Table is the highest-volume category.
3. Computers have the highest average selling price.
4. Revenue is diversified across multiple categories rather than concentrated in one segment.
5. Premium categories such as Watches & Gifts generate strong revenue despite lower sales volumes.
6. The marketplace relies on both high-volume and high-value categories to drive growth.

---

## Recommendations

### 1. Invest in High-Revenue Categories

Increase marketing efforts for:

* Health & Beauty
* Watches & Gifts
* Computers & Accessories

These categories generate strong returns and contribute substantially to total revenue.

### 2. Improve Cross-Selling Opportunities

Leverage high-volume categories such as Bed, Bath & Table to promote complementary products and increase average order value.

### 3. Expand Premium Product Offerings

Categories with high average prices offer opportunities for revenue growth even without large increases in order volume.

### 4. Monitor Category Diversification

The current revenue distribution is well balanced. Maintaining category diversity can reduce business risk and improve long-term stability.
