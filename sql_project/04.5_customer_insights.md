# Customer Analysis Insights

## Overview

This analysis examines customer behavior, geographic distribution, spending patterns, and customer retention across the Olist marketplace.

---

## Revenue by State

| State | Revenue |
| ----- | ------: |
| SP    | R$5.99M |
| RJ    | R$2.14M |
| MG    | R$1.87M |
| RS    |  R$891K |
| PR    |  R$811K |

### Insight

São Paulo (SP) is by far the most valuable market, generating nearly R$6 million in revenue and accounting for over one-third of total marketplace revenue.

The top three states (SP, RJ, and MG) together generate more than 60% of total revenue, indicating a strong geographic concentration of customers.

## Key Findings

1. São Paulo is the dominant market, generating nearly 38% of total revenue.
2. Major urban centers account for a significant share of sales activity.
3. Several smaller states exhibit higher average order values than larger markets.
4. Customer spending is highly concentrated among a small number of top customers.
5. Customer retention is very low, with only about 3% of customers making repeat purchases.
6. Revenue growth appears to depend heavily on acquiring new customers rather than increasing repeat purchases.

---

## Recommendations

### 1. Improve Customer Retention

Implement initiatives such as:

* Loyalty programs
* Personalized offers
* Email marketing campaigns
* Post-purchase engagement

Increasing repeat purchase rates could significantly boost revenue without requiring equivalent customer acquisition costs.

### 2. Focus on High-Value Regions

Continue investing in high-performing states:

* São Paulo
* Rio de Janeiro
* Minas Gerais

These markets generate the majority of marketplace revenue.

### 3. Target High-Spending Segments

States with high average order values may offer attractive opportunities for premium product promotions and targeted marketing.

### 4. Develop VIP Customer Programs

Identify and reward high-value customers to improve retention and maximize customer lifetime value.

### 5. Reduce Geographic Dependence

Expand customer acquisition efforts in underrepresented regions to reduce reliance on a few major states and improve long-term business resilience.
